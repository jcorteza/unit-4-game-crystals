Crystal Collector

You will be given a random score-goal at the start of the game. It is your goal to match this score using the four crystals. When clicked, each crytal adds a specific number to your current score.

Match your current score with the score-goal to win the game. If your score passes the score-goal, you lose the game.

During each new round, the crystals' values change. Figure out the value of each crystal by clicking on them.
